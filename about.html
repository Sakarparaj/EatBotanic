Our Story

Eat Botanic began with a simple idea: to create a dining experience where nature, taste, and ambiance unite in harmony. Nestled in the heart of Orange, NSW, at 302 Hill Street, our café and bar offer more than just delicious food. Inspired by the lush botanical elements within and the breathtaking sunsets visible from our outdoor spaces, we have crafted an environment that connects you to the natural beauty around you.

Since opening, we have been dedicated to delivering warmth, freshness, and creativity in every dish we serve. Whether you're here for a relaxing coffee, an evening drink, or a special gathering with friends and family, our goal is to make your visit as memorable as the landscape that surrounds us.

Our Goals

At Eat Botanic, our mission is to serve flavorful, exquisite food and beverages that highlight the finest locally sourced ingredients. We strive to combine modern culinary techniques with timeless classics, ensuring every meal is fresh, seasonal, and beautifully crafted. Our commitment to exceptional service ensures that each guest leaves feeling enriched by their experience and eager to return.

From casual meetups to intimate dinners and celebrations, we aim to make every occasion special by providing a welcoming, natural space where everyone feels at home.
